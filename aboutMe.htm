About Me

I hail from Waterbury, Connecticut also known as the Brass City where I spent much of my time gaming with my close pals and writing creatively until I graduated from Waterbury Arts Magnet School (WAMS) in June of 2016.

Prior to moving to New York with my mother, I was incredibly unmotivated to do anything with my life because I did not see much opportunity for growth within my environment in Waterbury.

While the living conditions where much more cramped, I flourished in the urban center of Ridgewood in Queens as my mother served as a critical role model for me. She has an incredible work ethic that inspired me to start working towards what I desired in life. While I don't know exactly what that is yet, I still find plenty of enjoyment working in various industries from retail, IT, and academic research, something I would've never known if not for my mother.

I was primiarly interested in mathematics where I first attended college at the New York City College of Technology. However, I realized that in the long run, Computer Science would give me more creative freedom in problem solving and as a result, I switched over to Computer Science before heading to the University of Michigan.
